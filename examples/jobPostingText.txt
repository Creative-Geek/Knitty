Job Summary:
We are looking for a highly motivated IT Intern to support our IT team in various tasks and gain hands-on experience in troubleshooting, system administration, and customer support. You will have the opportunity to enhance your skills in Windows OS installation, network troubleshooting, software support, data management, and more.
